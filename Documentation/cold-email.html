Cold email

High‑conversion, Ireland‑friendly outreach

Use this exact message for email. Adapt it slightly for Facebook, WhatsApp, or Instagram DMs. The goal is to sound local, specific, and low‑pressure.

Subject: Quick website idea for [Business Name] in [Town] Hi [Owner Name], I’m a local web developer here in Ireland, and I came across [Business Name] today. I noticed you don’t have a dedicated website yet — just your social media/Google listing. I help small Irish businesses get online with simple, fast, mobile‑friendly landing pages, and I’m currently offering a €149 starter website while building my portfolio. I actually put together a free demo preview of what your site could look like. Would you like me to send it on? No pressure at all — even if you’re just curious. Best regards, Laninya Design Resources Team E: Design.Resources@dr.com W: www.dr.com
Action for today:
Send this to 10 businesses from Google Maps. Look for those with no website or only a Facebook page.
