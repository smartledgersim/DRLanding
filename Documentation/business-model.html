Business model

From €0 to recurring income with one simple ladder

This is the exact model freelancers use to go from €0 → €1,000/month fast, especially in local markets like Ireland.

  • 1
    Lead Magnet (FREE)
    You offer: free audit, free demo screenshot, free consultation. This gets you conversations today.
  • 2
    Low‑Cost Entry (€10–€40)
    These are your “yes‑instantly” services: fix something, update something, add something. Once they pay you once, they trust you.
  • 3
    Core Offer (€149 Landing Page)
    This is your main income. You sell simple, fast, clean, no‑nonsense sites for trades, salons, cafés, and local shops.
  • 4
    Upsells (€15–€120)
    After the landing page, offer booking buttons, galleries, testimonials, SEO content, and extra pages.
  • 5
    Monthly Support (€20/hour)
    You become their “web person”. They call you for updates, fixes, admin, and content. This creates recurring income.
