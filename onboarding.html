Welcome to Laninya Design Resources

I’m excited to start building your new website. This onboarding page explains exactly how the process works, what I need from you, and what happens next.

What to Expect

  • A simple, friendly, transparent process.
  • Clear communication at every step.
  • No jargons, no confusion.
  • A website built for real Irish customers.

Step‑by‑Step Onboarding

  • Step 1: You send me your business details to show on the website
  • Step 2: Your homepage layout is created (draft)
  • Step 3: You review and request changes
  • Step 4: Your full site is built
  • Step 5: Final review + Launch

Step‑by‑Step Handover

"Manage it for You" Model
  • Step 1: Hosting: We host the site on our CloudFlare account.
  • Step 2: Domain: We help you buy a domain on a site like Namecheap.
  • Step 3: Connection: We "point" your domain to our CloudFlare project.
  • Step 4: The Result: We send you the link. We charge you a monthly Maintenance Fee of €15-€120 to keep it online and update for you.

Step‑by‑Step Handover

Option 2: The "Clean Handover" (Transferring Ownership)
  • Step 1: Client Account: We ask you to create a free account on CloudFlare.com.
  • Step 2: Transfer: In our CloudFlare dashboard, there is a button called "Transfer Site to another team."
  • Step 3: The Move: We type in your email address, and the entire website (and the domain connection) moves to your account.
  • Step 4: The Result: You now "own" the site, and we are no longer responsible for it.
